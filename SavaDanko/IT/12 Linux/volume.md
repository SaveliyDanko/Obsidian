#linux #filesystem 

---
A **volume** is a storage unit that holds data. In Linux, it usually refers to:
- A **physical disk partition** (e.g. `/dev/sda1`)
- A **logical volume** via LVM (e.g. `/dev/vg0/lv_home`)
- A **RAID device**
- A **loop device** (like an ISO image)
Think of a volume as a container for a filesystem.
