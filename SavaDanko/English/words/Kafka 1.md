
---
_landscape_ — пейзаж, ландшафт, природный вид местности
- I love taking pictures of the mountain landscape.
- The landscape around the lake is very peaceful.

---
_distribute_ — распределять
- The teacher distributed the worksheets to the students.
- The system distributes data between the servers.

---
_coarse-grained_ — крупнозернистый
Coarse-grained описывает что-то выполненное на крупном уровне, без мелких деталей.
- Our first version of the report is very coarse-grained
- The system provides only coarse-grained permission control.

---
_elasticity_ — эластичность, упругость
- This fabric has great elasticity and fits the body well.
- Rubber is known for its high elasticity.

---
_resilience_ — устойчивость
- The company demonstrated resilience during the crisis.
- She showed great resilience after losing her job.

---
_consistency_ — стабильность, согласованность
- The database ensures consistency between records.
- Consistency is key if you want to build good habits.

---
_promote_ — продвигать
- We need to promote our new product online.
- The event was well promoted on social media.

---
_applicability_ — применимость, возможность использования
- We need to check the applicability of this method to real projects.
- The solution has high applicability in software development.
